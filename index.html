Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart, cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holyday starter.

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500)g all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/5 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1 1/4 extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
    •

For the toppings:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on to
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though is not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make in a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully assorbed. You dont' need to actually kneed the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
